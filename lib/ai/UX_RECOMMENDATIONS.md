Retry" with a Retry button.\n- Permanent error: "We couldn't personalize this today. Upgrade to Premium for priority generation." with Support and Retry actions.\n\n4) Fallback Content\n- Always show a graceful fallback ritual (short, actionable) if AI generation fails. Mark it with a subtle "Generated by Zodian" tag.\n\n5) Loading Animations for Premium Experience\n- Use subtle motion, timed delays, and progressive reveal (title -> steps -> tips) to feel premium without blocking the user.\n\n6) Analytics hooks\n- Track: load_start, load_success, load_fallback, load_error, retry_click, upgrade_cta. Send contextual metadata (signs, weekday).\n\n7) Accessibility\n- Ensure skeletons are accessible to assistive tech (announce "Loading ritual") and error states have descriptive text and focus management.\n\n8) Rate-limiting UX\n- If generation is rate-limited, surface a friendly message and a cooldown timer.
